# MindWell Hub | Digital Mental Health Literacy Hub 🧠🌱

**MindWell Hub** is a specialized digital platform designed for higher education students to enhance mental health literacy, reduce social stigma, and provide accessible pathways for professional support.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
Mental health challenges often go unnoticed due to academic pressure and lack of awareness. MindWell Hub bridges this gap by offering a student-centered platform that provides self-assessment tools, educational modules, and peer support networks.

### The Problem
* **Awareness Gap:** Students often struggle to recognize early symptoms of stress or anxiety.
* **Stigma:** Fear of judgment prevents students from seeking help.
* **Fragmentation:** Difficulty in finding consolidated, trustworthy mental health resources.

### The Solution
An integrated web ecosystem that empowers students with knowledge, allows for private peer interaction, and streamlines the connection between students and mental health professionals (MHP).

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔐 User Management & Authentication
* **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Distinct specialized gateways and dashboards for Students, Administrators, and Mental Health Professionals.
* **Secure Sessions:** Implementation of secure login and registration protocols using Java/JSP logic.

### 👥 Peer Support Forum
* **Safe Discussions:** A collaborative forum for sharing experiences on topics like stress management and self-care.
* **Privacy-First:** Anonymity toggle feature allowing students to post and reply without revealing their identities to encourage honest dialogue.

### 📊 Self-Assessment & Wellness
* **Interactive Screening:** Tools for daily mood tracking, stress assessments, and anxiety screening.
* **Resource Feed:** A structured dashboard providing wellness tips and personalized resource suggestions.

### 🤖 AI Interface Prototype
* **Smart Guidance:** A UI-driven chatbot assistant designed to simulate real-time guidance for students and clinical resource drafting for professionals.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **Backend:** Java (Servlet), JSP (JavaServer Pages)
* **Database:** SQL (MySQL) for relational data management and secure user profiles
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
* **Methodology:** Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

---

## 🎨 System Architecture
The system is built on a **Model-View-Controller (MVC)** inspired architecture to ensure a clean separation between the database logic (SQL), the server-side processing (Java), and the user interface (JSP).
